BLOG #1 11/18/2022

I Love One Piece

Well hello again, it's me, Mousa. Now as some of you may or may not know I have been on a short little hiatus from the internet (well social media). During my break, I have decided to catch up on many things. I have decided to read more (currently taking book recommendations), self-care through exercise and therapy as well as eating better, and lastly I have been watching a lot of anime. Mostly Naruto, Mob Psycho, and now possibly one of my new favorite anime One Piece. through a mixture of reading the manga and watching the episodes at night, I have been able to get to the middle of the Arabasta arc (episode 117 in the anime). Without a doubt, I have never felt so touched before emotionally when watching tv. Oda is able to craft a world that is so inviting and truly encourages me to push harder and be better. When I see Luffy proclaim his dreams of being the pirate king I find myself gaining confidence in my own dreams.

Of course, to truly achieve those dreams you don't only need confidence in yourself but you also need confidence and trust in those around you. This is where I personally fell short in the past and I apologize I should trust some of yall more. My distrust didn't come from a false superiority but more so a fear of nobody being able to truly relate to me. Thankfully though seeing the straw hat pirates gain each other's trust I felt myself being more open vicariously. Every Straw hat I have met (so far) has taught me a valuable lesson, Luffy taught me I can take on the world with a good attitude and a good group of people. Zoro taught me the power of will and self-discipline and at times during my hiatus, I truly felt like him. Nami taught me how to trust people you initially didn't like at all. Sanji taught me about the purity of positive emotions like love and bonds. Tony Tony Chopper taught me about fighting for a cause despite the odds like a true pirate. IDGAF bout how cringe this shit sounds A MF LIKE I actually cried watching this show so many times. When Luffy called on Nami in arlong park, Chopper's backstory, hell even the damn dog from the first arc had me crying. Oh wait I forgot Usopp... ermm I like dude but he be lying too much tbh lol I was already good at lying before though so maybe he gets braver later in the show. I love all the straw hat pirates and I will not forget what they represent to me.





Tying it all together now. I am back on the internet now but not as an escape from my reality which had only caused my existential angst and fears, but more so as a way to truly solidify my bonds to the outside world. The truth of the matter is I was good alone I found a job now, I am talking to a therapist right now and I have been on top of my assignments, while this may be good I am in search of better. What I truly value in my life is an adventure, and while I don't truly know where it will take me I am sure as hell to enjoy the company and stories along the way. In the end, it isn't the one with the most power who is the happiest, it is the one who is the freest. I am in search of my freedom and I can't achieve the goal without my crew. and there will always be room for you if you want to be my friend. - Monkey D.
